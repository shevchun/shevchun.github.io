What is an Aikuchi Sword? H3 Bold

The aikuchi is a type of Japanese sword that was commonly used by samurai warriors during the feudal period of Japan. This sword is characterized by its unique hilt, which has no guard and a simple design. The blade of the aikuchi is also typically shorter than other types of Japanese swords, making it ideal for close-quarter combat. The name "aikuchi" is derived from the Japanese word "aikuchi-gatana," which means "sword with a wrapped handle."
