Who we are?

Kegglers is a company founded in late 2020. However, our endeavor began back in 2018 when the founder’s sister who is a psychotherapist encountered many women who experienced sexual trauma. We envisioned helping women across the world celebrate their femininity and to address sexual health.

We aim to break barriers as sexual issues often remain unaddressed in this day and age. Women should feel empowered, connected to each other, and comfortable voicing out their concerns or opinions on sexual health.

We are here to support, guide, and inspire you through your journey of self-discovery no matter how different it is. With the belief that every individual is unique and beautiful, Kegglers aim to help women become more conscious of their value in the community and family.

Our goal is to break taboos and patriarchal views through the power of education. We hope you tap into our resources – from our blog posts and information hubs so that by learning, you can help raise public awareness. We look forward to having you join our community of like-minded individuals who encourage and motivate each other!

Our Approach

At Kegglers, we believe in holistic medicine. With a whole-body approach, we believe that health consists of wellness of the mind, body and soul. We take into consideration the combination of traditional, complementary, and alternative medicine that has been around for hundreds if not thousands of years along with modern medicine when choosing our products.

We Educate

At Kegglers, we believe that education is key and provides the knowledge needed to transform lives. We realize that there are many websites that sell sexual health related products without providing useful information on how to safely use them. By working closely with professionals in the field and our content writers, we strive to provide accurate information that is beneficial for our clients.

Our history
2018
August

Our founder’s sister, a psychotherapist realized that many of her patients were women who experienced sexual trauma. She brought up the idea of increasing awareness regarding sexual health, mental health, and empowering women.

October

The research for products that can benefit women physically and spiritually began. Planning on how the project should be implemented also started.

2019
March

We started connecting with yoga practitioners and physicians to gain insight on our products.

July

During our research, we realize the importance of education in helping women become more confident and self-assured.

November

We started planning for brand implementation.

2020
January

The project has reached the stage where production of our items started. Our editorial team also began producing content for our community.

July

As we try to fit our final piece of the puzzle into the picture, we worked tirelessly for the launch of our website.

November

Our long-awaited moment, going live with Kegglers.com
