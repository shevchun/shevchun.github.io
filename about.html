About Ensure

Ensure Endpoint Technologies Inc. is a subsidiary of AlertSec Inc.

Ensure Endpoint Technologies Inc., founded in 2021, is a fully incorporated subsidiary of AlertSec Inc. AlertSec, the leader in cloud-managed endpoint encryption (AES-256, certification to FIPS 140-2, Common Criteria EAL4 and BITS), was founded in 2007.

Encrypting endpoints inspired us to develop a tool to assess whether a third-party device is fully secure or not. This is the core of Ensure Endpoint Technologies – a platform for policy based device controls for antivirus, data encryption, firewall and operating system updates.
Ebba Blitz
CO-Founder and CEO of Ensure Endpoint Technologies Inc.
Benefits Ensure

Ensure Endpoint and AlertSec share IT infrastructure and a 24/7 helpdesk. The Ensure Endpoint/AlertSec IT infrastructure and processes have passed the vBSIMM (Vendor Building Security In Maturity Model) assessment, in order to securely provide software services to US carriers.

The Ensure Endpoint/AlertSec helpdesk is certified to ISO 9001. Ensure Endpoint’s cyber security policy includes pen testing protocols, as well as encryption of data, multi factor authentication, secure back up procedures, swift alarm and incident response procedures. Ensure Endpoint Technologies Inc. is listed in the FINRA Compliance Vendor Directory.

User access

Ensure Endpoint Technologies Inc. does not hold any non-public information, or data, that can be linked to an individual using our services. The Ensure Endpoint assessment software does not have any means of collecting, or extracting, any personal information from the device it assesses.
