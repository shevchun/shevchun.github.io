Our take on Sombra Joven

Sombra Agave de Oaxaca was born from the mission to preserve the passion, traditions, and culture embodied in Mezcal production with the help of Eduardo and Julio Mestres.

Sombra was born from the mission to preserve the passion, traditions, and culture embodied in Mezcal production. The company spent decades sampling Mezcal from villages across native Mexico.

Local Maestros Mezcaleros use methods passed down through generations to produce authentic, super-premium Mezcal.
