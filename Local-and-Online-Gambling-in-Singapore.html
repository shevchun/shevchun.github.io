History of Gambling in Singapore

  • 1843 The Singapore Sporting Club holds the first horse racing. It is later known as the Singapore Turf Club.

  • 1843 Horse racing officially opens to the public.
  • After independence The government tried to curb illegal gambling
  • 1968 Singapore Pools Private Limited became the sole legal operator of lottery. There are now more than 280 Singapore Pools outlets spread across Singapore.
