Blackjack or twenty-one is a card game between one or more players and the dealer. It is a simple card game that involves skill and chance. It is important for players to note that they do not compete against each other but instead, playing against the dealer.

Blackjack is played with one or more decks of cards. It is the most widely played casino banking game in the world. Players and the dealer are dealt two cards. Face cards have a value of 10 while aces can be worth one or eleven. The rest of the cards are worth their pip value (2 to 10).

The goal is to reach a value of 21 without exceeding 21. If your sum is higher than the dealer, you win the hand. A hand that hits 21 immediately wins the hand.
