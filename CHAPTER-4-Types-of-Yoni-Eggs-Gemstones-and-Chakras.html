Undrilled vs Drilled Yoni Eggs

Undrilled yoni eggs are entirely smooth without a drilled hole. This means it can be removed by pushing the egg out using the pelvic floor muscles. It is much easier to clean.

Drilled yoni eggs have a small hole drilled into them. This allows the user to attach a disposable string like an unwaxed dental floss. Users tug on the string to help remove the egg. Drilled eggs are often preferred by beginners. However, drilled eggs require special attention during cleaning.

 

The Seven Chakras

We start with some basic information about the seven chakras. This helps you understand how different gemstones have crystal healing properties.

 

What Does Chakra Mean?

Chakra comes from a Sanskrit word. It means “wheel” or “disk”. It is believed that there are 7 of these main energy centers in the body. Each chakra corresponds to different organs and nerve bundles. Chakras need to stay balanced or open. If blocked, it can result in emotional or physical issues.

 
Root Chakra (Muladhara)

The root chakra or Muladhara represents our foundation. It is located at the base of the spine. It helps you to withstand challenges and to feel grounded. For this reason, the root chakra is linked with your sense of stability and security. If it is blocked, the person can feel threatened and insecure.

Color: Red
Element: Earth
Sacral Chakra (Svadhistana)

Located below your belly button, the sacral chakra is responsible for creative and sexual energy. It is also linked with fertility. It plays a role in how you relate emotions and the emotions of others. If blocked, it can cause the person that they are lacking control in their lives. Some are unable to find bodily pleasure or feel true intimacy.

Color: Orange
Element: Water
Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

The solar plexus chakra or Manipura is also known as the navel chakra. It is linked to confidence and control of life. It is also a representation of clear judgement, maturity, fairness, and authority. If blocked, it can cause low confidence, dependency on others, and self-doubt. Those with open Manipura can express themselves and be assertive.

Color: Yellow
Element: Fire
Heart Chakra (Anahata)

Located near the heart, the heart chakra is linked to the ability to show compassion and love. Symbolized by a 12-petaled sacred lotus, it is the bridge between the upper and lower chakras. This means it connects spirituality (upper chakras) and materialism (lower chakras). If blocked, it can result in difficulty opening up to others. The affected person may be isolated, lonely, and unable to forgive or trust.

Color: Green
Element: Air
Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

Located in the throat, the throat chakra can be linked with voice. It controls the ability to communicate. If blocked, it can manifest as chronic anxiety and inability to express oneself.

Color: Turquoise / Light Blue
Element: Music / Sound
Third Eye Chakra (Ajna)

Also known as the guru chakra, it is located between the eyes. It is linked with intuition, gut instinct, and imagination. It controls the ability to see the big picture. Those with an open third eye chakra may have visions and better intuition.
