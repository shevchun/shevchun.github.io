Description:
The Cutlass, a particular type of sword, has roots in Europe. It is closely associated with pirates, sea sailors, and buccaneers. Noted for its straightforward, single-edged design, this sword was easy to manufacture in large volumes. Its practicality was evident in daily tasks such as cutting ropes or trees. The Cutlass design excelled in close-combat situations, making it the perfect choice for ship deck battles. Even today, some world navies continue to employ it.
