Hishigami

The standard model that is ready to ship comes without hishigami. Hishigami are small folded paper triangles inserted into the tsuka-maki wrap to maintain its shape and provide a firmer grip. They are usually preferred and recommended for use in tameshigiri (cutting practice). Hishigami Pro is an advanced version, made from specially treated paper, which makes the wrap even tighter.

Product details

SKU: THK6683-01

This Ogami Itto Katana is inspired by the Lone Wolf and Cub series, created by Kazuo Koike and Goseki Kojima. It tells of Ogami Itto, a shogun's executioner who uses a battle sword to seek revenge on the Yagyu clan who falsely accused him, causing him to take the path of an assassin

As a tribute to Ogami Itto, Dragon King has faithfully reproduced his katana using forged steel. The design features an awabi tsuba (distinctive blackened abalone shell guard) that contrasts beautifully with the polished fuchi (hilt collar) and kashira (pommel cap), both etched with Japanese Dogwood blossoms. Silver dragon menuki (pegs) secure the blade to the tsuka (handle), providing added strength and stability.

The tsuka is wrapped in black cotton ito over white same (rayskin). The katana comes sheathed in a black ribbed saya (scabbard) with a silver steel kojiri (scabbard end cap) etched with Japanese Dogwood blossoms. The koiguchi (mouth of the scabbard) and kurikata (cord knob) are crafted from black buffalo horn.

This katana is a faithful reproduction of Ogami Itto's weapon. Fans and collectors of the series will surely appreciate this stunning masterpiece.
